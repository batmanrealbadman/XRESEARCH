Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and earnings

How do I get paid for my research?

We process payments monthly through bank transfers or mobile money. You'll need to set up your payment method in your account settings once your earnings reach our minimum payout threshold of ₦5,000.

Is there any limit to how much I can earn?

No limits! The more downloads your research receives, the more you earn. Some of our top researchers earn over ₦50,000 monthly from popular papers.

What types of research can I upload?

We accept quality academic research across all disciplines - from sciences to humanities. All submissions go through a quality review to ensure they meet our academic standards.

Can I upload research I've co-authored?

Yes, but all co-authors must agree to the upload. You'll need to specify the earnings distribution among authors during the upload process.
